Scenic dining

Welcome to The View

Indulge in the harmonious fusion of authentic local cuisine at our on-site restaurant and bar. With a picturesque ocean backdrop, our dining venue offers breakfast, lunch, and dinner, while the bar remains open throughout the day until late, ensuring a delightful culinary experience from dawn till dusk.

Western classics and Asian favorites

Our skilled chefs have meticulously curated a menu of exquisite traditional dishes, thoughtfully complemented by a selection of Western favorites. Be sure to savor our homemade pizzas, expertly crafted in our wood-burning oven for a truly authentic culinary experience

We take pride in using only the finest locally sourced ingredients in all our dishes. For reservations, please call us at +99 955 888 500, or feel free to reserve in person at your convenience.

Rejuvenating spa experience

Jai Spa

Escape to a serene and rejuvenating sanctuary to fulfill all your wellness desires. At The Jai Spa, indulge in a variety of treatments including wraps, facials, traditional massages, and foot reflexology, all customized to cater to your specific needs. Let our seasoned therapists guide you on a journey to relaxation and detoxification, allowing you to pamper yourself to the fullest.

All-natural ingredients

At our hotel and spa, we exclusively utilize all-natural ingredients and products meticulously tailored to suit your individual needs.

Conveniently nestled within the resort, the Jai Spa offers a romantic retreat for couples or a tranquil escape for those seeking a quick massage to unwind. Explore our couples and friends’ packages, designed to help you relax and rejuvenate alongside your favorite person.

Activities

Diverse excursions

From breathtaking beaches and exhilarating water sports to enriching cultural experiences like temple visits and exploring local markets, the island offers something for every traveler. Food enthusiasts can delight in authentic local cuisine, while those craving relaxation can luxuriate in our opulent spa. Adventure seekers can embark on hikes through verdant forests or embark on elephant safaris, while night owls can revel in the island's vibrant nightlife.

Guest Relations

Uncover the essence of the island through exclusive insider recommendations.

With profound familiarity of this breathtaking island, our dedicated Guest Relations team is poised to offer tailor-made suggestions for dining, entertainment, and activities. Always at your service, they stand ready to assist with any needs, whether it's arranging transportation or securing reservations at exclusive dining establishments. With an unwavering commitment to exceptional service and meticulous attention to detail, our team ensures that every guest enjoys an unforgettable stay.
